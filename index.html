How it works?


Shape and Feature Changes are not only easily Recognizable by the Trained Eye, but also more Accurately Recognized and Measured by new Computerized Techniques known as Computer Vision and Machine Learning. These Features are more readily Detected through Specialized Filters and then more Accurately Measured using the Digitized Photograph.

There are 3 Features Per Tooth that we are Analyzing

  • The Shape of the Tooth
  • The Shape of the Infundibulum (Cup)
  • The Shape of the Pulp Chamber (Dental Star)

We monitor the Change in the Shape, Angle, and Dimensions of the Tooth along with the Galvayne's Groove on the Labial Aspect of theIntermediate and Corner Incisors (16 Total Measurements).

Our Database of 500 Horses is evenly distributed between age groups and the Bounding Boxes (the 52 Indivdual Measurements of Each Horse related to its Corresponding Age), are used to Train the Model for Age Prediction via Machine Learning.
